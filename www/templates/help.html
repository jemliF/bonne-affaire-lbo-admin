1. What is an offer?

An offer is a market opportunity provided by a company to a limited number of subscribers during restricted period of time.

2. What is a business category?

A business category is a group of related or similar products.

3. How to create an offer?

Offer creation is restricted to our website solution, go to our website, log in, create your offers and send them to administration for validation.

4. How to update an existing offer?

Usually, when you send an offer, you cannot change its content.

5. What is validity duration of an offer?

Validity duration is a date interval in which you offer is visible to the client. Out of this period, you offer is expired.

6. How to update my account informations?

Select "Account" menu to customize your account informations.
